My name is Yiru Chen (陈一茹 in Chinese). I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Columbia University advised by Prof. Eugene Wu. My research interests are database system, human data interaction, visualization, and machine learning.

Prior to Columbia, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science SUMMA CUM LAUDE and Economics at Peking University in 2018. In PKU, I used to work on topic models and in-database machine learing with Prof. Bin Cui.

I am awarded the Google PhD Fellowship in Structure Data and Databases Management.
